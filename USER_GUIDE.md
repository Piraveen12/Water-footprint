Welcome to the **Water Footprint Tracker**! This application helps you understand the hidden water cost of your daily items. Here is how to use the features we have built.

## 1. üîç Scanning & Searching (Main Tab)

This is the default screen when you open the app.

*   **Scan an Item**:
    1.  Click the **"Scan Item"** button to open your camera.
    2.  Point it at a product (e.g., an apple, a shirt, a bottle) and capture.
    3.  Alternatively, click **"Upload Image"** to choose a file from your device.
*   **Search by Text**:
    *   Type an item name (e.g., "Rice", "Jeans", "Paper") in the search bar and press **Enter**.
*   **View Results**:
    *   The app will analyze the product using AI.
    *   You will see the **Water Footprint Card** showing:
        *   Total Liters of water used.
        *   Breakdown (Green, Blue, Grey water).
        *   Severity (Low/Medium/High).
        *   Regional comparisons.

## 2. üìä Personal Tracker (My Tracker Tab)

Track your history and get personalized advice.

1.  Click the **"My Tracker"** button at the top of the app.
2.  **View History**: You will see a list of every item you have scanned, along with a chart showing your recent usage.
3.  **Analyze Habits (AI)**:
    *   Click the **"Analyze My Habits"** button.
    *   The AI will review your history and tell you:
        *   Which items are consuming the most water.
        *   Specific habits you should change.
        *   Personalized alternatives.

## 3. üåê Local Languages

*   Look for the **Language Dropdown** in the top-right corner.
*   Select **Hindi**, **Tamil**, **Malayalam**, **Kannada**, or **Telugu**.
*   The analysis results (Item name, description, tips) will be translated into your chosen language.
